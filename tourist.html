Tourist Spots

Mt. Lammin

Home of arabica coffee plantation and rocky mountain cafe. Mt. Lammin is a Twenty four kilometres trail run-hike and it takes four hours going to peak and three hours for descending.

Sabo Dam

Dubbed as the “summer capital” of the province, the Sabo dam and a hanging bridge serve as major attractions for picnickers and nature lovers.
